# assets

## Profile photo (REPLACE ME)

Drop your real photo here as:

    assets/profile.jpg

- Square image recommended (e.g. 600×600 px or larger).
- The hero `<img>` in `index.html` points to `assets/profile.jpg`.
- Until you add it, a decorative placeholder + an animated "Replace with your
  photo" badge is shown. Once `profile.jpg` loads successfully, the badge
  automatically disappears (handled in `script.js`).

Other files here:

- `profile-placeholder.svg` — fallback artwork (you can delete it after adding
  your own photo, it is optional).
