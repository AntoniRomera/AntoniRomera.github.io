About

Engineer at the intersection of AI & infrastructure

I'm Antoni — I like turning fast-moving AI ideas into things that actually run in production. That means provider-agnostic agent frameworks (Gemini, Claude and Ollama behind one interface), self-hosted LLM stacks you fully control, and the boring-but-essential cloud plumbing — VPCs, EKS, RDS — captured as reusable Terraform.

Recently I've been wiring AI agents to real systems through the Model Context Protocol, driving my repos from my phone over Tailscale, and untangling enterprise SSO across .NET and Java with Keycloak.
